DL4EO packages is a streamline process to acquire sentinel-1, Sentinel-2, Slope, Elevation and spectral index (NDWI) to generate image-pair labels for any EO segmentation task. This tool perform AOI selection, downloading, preprocessing (e.g., RTC/NDWI), tiling, normalization, and split creation for training and evluating any deep learning model.
